About Sumikko Gurashi Land

Nestled in Tokyo, Japan, Sumikko Gurashi Land is a dreamy destination where fans of the shy little corner-dwellers can immerse themselves in their pastel paradise! Enjoy themed rides, cafes, and photo spots inspired by your favorite characters like Shirokuma, Penguin, Tonkatsu, Tokage, and Neko.

Highlights & Fun Facts

Themed Rides

Each area is inspired by different characters — ride through Neko’s Garden or float in Shirokuma’s Snowy Boat!

Cozy Café

Try adorable pastries shaped like Tonkatsu and friends, served in pastel teacups.

Souvenir Shop

Find exclusive plushies and collectibles only available at Sumikko Gurashi Land!

Location

Located in Shibuya, Tokyo just a few stops from the famous Scramble Crossing!
